Sputnik launched on october 4th 1987 by the Soviet Union. Sputnik was the worlds first ever man made satellite.
Sputnik was about the size of a beach ball with 4 antenna protruding from it. While it did not have any sensors on board sputnik was able to show scientists the density of the different parts of the atmosphere (based on how it fell back to earth) and how radio waves travel through the atmosphere.
